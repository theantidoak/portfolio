The Role

Tommy Doak is a front-end web developer in Paris, France. He currently contracts for an American national college and university ranking start-up called Academicinfluence.com, where he handles UX, UI, video and image content, the creation of website tools, database management, and digital marketing campaigns.

The Backstory

Tommy grew up in Chattanooga, Tennessee and graduated from the University of Georgia with a BA in Marketing. He worked as an alarm technician in Halifax, Nova Scotia and door-to-door salesman in Chicago before moving to Barcelona to complete a Teach English as a Foreign Language (TEFL) course. He taught English as Second Lanaguage (ESL) at a language school in Shanghai and public schools in Ho Chi Minh City. He also taught ESL online for a Chinese company while living in Colombia, Mexico, Germany, and France. Between and alongside jobs in the ESL industry, Tommy worked in a salmon fishery in King Salmon, a deckhand on two commercial fishing vessels in Alaska, a headhunter for a cruise line recruiting agency, and a construction laborer in Antarctica.
